What is a link shortener ?

A link shortener is a tool that transforms your long links into shorter ones. Our Shortify™ tool can drastically shorten your long links and still reliably direct to the same web pages. To use our link shortener, the user only has to copy and paste their long link into our tool and we condense it into a more shareable link.
